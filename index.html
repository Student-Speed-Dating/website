Our Mission & Philosophy

We’re here to make dating less casual and more meaningful. Apps feel noisy and transactional—too many dishonest interactions. Our setting is comfortable, convenient, and respectful by design.

Stories over swipes

Share a fun fact, show a photo of your hobby, or tell a quick story. That’s how real connections start.

Host-guided & safe

Light prompts, respectful timing, camera-on, and verification to keep the vibe friendly.

How it works

  1. 1 Pick your chapter and RSVP for the weekly online event.
  2. 2 Join short, timed, one-on-one video chats with verified students.
  3. 3 Share a funny story, interests, hobbies—be yourself.
  4. 4 After the event, choose who you’d like to keep chatting with.
  5. 5 Mutual “yes” only → we privately introduce your social handles.

Why it works

  • Structured, low-pressure chats beat endless swipes.
  • City chapters make meeting up later simple.
  • Consent-first: no match, no info shared.
  • Privacy and safety are built-in from day one.

FAQ

Is this a dating app?

No. It’s a weekly, host-facilitated virtual event with consent-first matching.

How are matches made?

You privately mark “yes/no” after the event. Only mutual “yes” pairs are introduced by exchanging social media details.

How do you verify students?

Before attending, provide either two active social accounts or a valid student card. We verify eligibility only and don’t share your info.

Do I have to keep my camera on?

Yes. Camera is required for the entire event. No camera = no attendance.
