How it works

  • Pick your chapter and RSVP for the weekly online event.
  • Join short, timed, one-on-one video chats with other verified students (18–27).
  • Share a funny story, interests, hobbies—be yourself.
  • After the event, choose who you’d like to keep chatting with.
  • Mutual “yes” only: we privately introduce your social media details.

Why it works

  • Structured, low-pressure chats beat endless swipes.
  • City chapters make meeting up later simple.
  • Consent-first: no match, no info shared.
  • Privacy and safety are built-in from day one.

Eligibility & Rules

  • Age 18–27.
  • Verified student or recent graduate (provide either two active social media accounts or a valid student card before attending).
  • Camera on for the full event — no camera, no attendance.
  • Be respectful — zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination.
  • No screenshots or recordings.
  • Mutual consent required before any contact details are shared.

FAQ

Is this a dating app?

No. It’s a weekly, host-facilitated virtual event with consent-first matching.

How are matches made?

You privately mark “yes/no” after the event. Only mutual “yes” pairs are introduced by exchanging social media details.

How do you verify students?

Before attending, provide either two active social media accounts or a valid student card. We verify eligibility only and do not share your info.

Do I have to keep my camera on?

Yes. Camera is required for the entire event. No camera = no attendance.
